Common Questions
FAQ
How long does a typical project take? +

Small items (single glasses, gifts) usually take 1–2 weeks. Larger projects like mirrors or event sets typically take 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. If you're on a tight timeline, let me know — I'll do my best to accommodate rush orders.

Can I provide my own glass or glassware? +

Absolutely. If you've got a specific piece or set you want etched, I'm happy to work with it. I can also source glass and glassware for you if you don't have something in mind — I work with several suppliers and can get most styles.

Do you do installation for larger pieces? +

Yes — for local Petaluma and Sonoma County jobs, I offer on-site measurement and installation for mirrors and panels. I'll come out, measure your space, and install the finished piece. For projects outside the county, I arrange careful shipping with full insurance.

What if I don't have a design in mind? +

That's totally fine — most people don't. I'll work with you to develop a concept. Just tell me what the piece is for, where it's going, and any ideas you have (even vague ones), and I'll take it from there with mockups and proofs until we land on something you love.

Do you work outside of Sonoma County? +

Yes. While I'm based in Petaluma and most of my clients are in Sonoma County, I ship throughout California and beyond. For larger or fragile pieces, I use custom crating and fully insured shipping. Event work is primarily local.

Have a Project in Mind?

Tell me what you're thinking. Free estimates, no pressure, and I usually respond within 24 hours.
